Ferret

Key features: Ferrets have a white undercoat with darker hairs on top. The usual colours are black, white or grey with a noticeably darker tail and a little black mask across their eyes and nose.

Rat

Key features: The common or ship rat is the most prevalent rat in New Zealand. It has a tail longer than its body and ears that cover its eyes.

Stoat

Key Features: Stoats are similar in appearance to weasels but are larger.there is no blending of fur colour where the reddish brown fur meets the white of the belly. Stoats also have a distinctive black tip at the end of their tail. In cold areas they can sometimes have a white fur coat.
